# 🏎️ LEGO F1 IKEA Frame Generator

A fan-made **web app** for generating print-ready PDF backgrounds for **LEGO F1 Speed Champions** sets displayed in **IKEA SANNAHED frames**.

---

## 🖼️ About the Project

Custom-designed print backgrounds intended to complement LEGO F1 Speed Champions sets placed inside the deep IKEA SANNAHED frame. The frame's 6 cm depth makes it perfect for a diorama-style display with the LEGO car mounted on a stud plate inside.

The app lets you combine any team designs into a single print-ready PDF — either for the **25×25 cm** (2 slots) or **50×50 cm** (8 slots) frame size.

## 🖥️ How to Use

1. **Open** the web app
2. **Select frame size** — 25×25 cm (2 slots) or 50×50 cm (8 slots)
3. **Click a slot** in the frame preview to select it
4. **Pick a team** from the list
5. **Repeat** for all slots
6. Click **Export PDF** and save the file
7. Print the exported PDF and follow the instructions below

---

## 🖨️ Print Instructions

1. Print the exported PDF at **100% scale** — do not scale to fit page
2. Recommended paper: **matte photo paper 250 g/m²**
3. **Trim** along the crop marks
4. **Cut out the center hole** in each slot — this is where the LEGO stud plate sits

---

## 🔧 Assembly Instructions

**What you need:**
- Printed and trimmed background
- IKEA SANNAHED frame
- LEGO 2×3 stud plate (one per slot)
- Double-sided tape (strong)
- LEGO F1 Speed Champions car

**Steps:**

1. Open the frame and remove the white backing paper inside
2. Remove the mat board (the thick paper insert) — you won't need it
3. Place your trimmed print on the back panel from the display side
4. Apply strong double-sided tape to the bottom of the LEGO 2×3 stud plate
5. Press the stud plate into the cut-out hole and fix it in place
6. Attach the F1 car onto the stud plate
7. Reassemble the frame and hang or stand it
